About Us

Empowering Communities Since 2023

Funke Helping Hand Foundation was established with a singular vision: to create meaningful change in communities across West Africa. What started as a small initiative has grown into a movement, touching lives and bringing hope to thousands.

We believe in sustainable solutions that empower individuals and communities to build brighter futures for themselves—not just temporary aid, but lasting transformation.

Our Programs

Initiatives That Change Lives

We focus on four core areas where we believe we can create the most impactful, sustainable change.

Education & Literacy

Providing school supplies, scholarships, and literacy programs to ensure every child has access to quality education.

Learn More

Healthcare Access

Organizing medical outreach programs, providing essential medicines, and supporting healthcare facilities.

Learn More

Women Empowerment

Skills training, micro-finance opportunities, and mentorship programs for women's economic independence.

Building infrastructure, clean water solutions, and sustainable programs for lasting improvements.
